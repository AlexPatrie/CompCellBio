Upcoming CCB Workshops 2021


22nd Computational Cell Biology Workshop

The 22nd Annual Workshop on Compututional Cell Biology will be sponsored by the Center for Cell Analyis and Modeling (CCAM) at the University of Connecticut School of Medicine on June 21-23, 2021. It will be an intense on-line event designed to enable cell biologists and biophysicists to develop models of their experimental system using Virtual Cell, COPASI, and SpringSaLaD software systems. Please see CCB Workshop for more information.
